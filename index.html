About us

We are the Brunel University Spanish Society and our goal is to celebrate Spain and its culture. We plan to run regular events throughout the year ranging from weekly sports meet-ups for badminton and basketball, days out for amusement parks, paintballing, go-karting, movies and nights out for parties, clubs, dinners together and even our annual end-of-year trip.
